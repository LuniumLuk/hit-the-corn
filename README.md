# Hit the Corn

a simple iOS AR game implemented with SwiftUI, RealityKit and ARKit

<img src="./images/corn.png" width="240px">

## Introduction

1. open the App
2. scan your surrounding area
3. hit the corn once they emerge from the ground and have fun!
